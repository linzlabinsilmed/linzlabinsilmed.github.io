About us

We are a research team at Johannes Kepler University and the Johann Radon Institute for Computational and Applied Mathematics, located in Linz, Austria. Our mission is to advance medical interventions through innovative in-silico modelling techniques. Our current research focuses on enhancing the personalization of ablation treatments and ECMO interventions using patient-specific modelling. Our ambition is to ensure that patients receive the most effective and personalized care for improved health outcomes. More information: https://www.jku.at/en/institute-for-mathematical-methods-in-medicine-and-data-based-modeling/ https://www.oeaw.ac.at/ricam/research/groups/group-gerardo-giorda
