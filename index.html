Linz Lab for In Silico Medical Interventions

About

We are a research team at Johannes Kepler University and the Johann Radon Institute for Computational and Applied Mathematics, located in Linz, Austria.

Our mission is to advance medical interventions through innovative in-silico modelling techniques. Our current research focuses on enhancing the personalization of ablation treatments and ECMO interventions using patient-specific modelling.

Our ambition is to ensure that patients receive the most effective and personalized care for improved health outcomes.
