At the moment we don't have any fully funded positions available. However, we are always looking for skilled and enthusiastic students or junior researchers to join our team. If our research aligns with your interests and expertise, please email us, including your CV and a short explanation of your motivation. You can find a list of sample projects [here], but we are also open to your own project ideas, provided they fit our group's research focus.
